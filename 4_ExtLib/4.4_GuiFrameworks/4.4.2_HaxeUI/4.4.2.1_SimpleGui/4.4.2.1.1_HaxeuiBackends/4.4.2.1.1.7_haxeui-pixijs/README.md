haxe-basics: haxeui-pixijs
=========================

Requre packages [haxeui-core](https://github.com/haxeui/haxeui-core), [haxeui-pixijs](https://github.com/haxeui/haxeui-pixijs) and [pixi-haxe](https://github.com/pixijs/pixi-haxe).
